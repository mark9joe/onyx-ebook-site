Key Themes in Onyx Storm

The Power of Choice

The central theme of Onyx Storm explores whether destiny is predetermined or if individuals can forge their own paths. This philosophical question plays out through:

  • The protagonist's evolving relationship with their Onyx dragon
  • Critical decisions that affect the rebellion's outcome
  • The revelation of ancient prophecies and their interpretations

The Nature of Power

Yarros examines different forms of power throughout the narrative:

  • The raw power of dragons and their storm-summoning abilities
  • Political power within the rider hierarchy
  • The power of knowledge and ancient secrets
  • Personal power gained through self-discovery
